Violin Accessories

Here are pictures of a few violin related accessories:



This is a violin case. they make different types in many shapes, sizes, materials and colors. Cases hold the instrument and accessories. Some cases have more room to store accessories than others.


This is called a violin bow. Its how players "pull" the sound out of the instrument. It is traditionally made out of horse hair and wood. Today you can find bows made out of other more durable materials like carbon fiber.


This is called rosin. its made from tree sap and so it is sticky. This is applied to the bow to cause friction on the strings, causing them to vibrate (vibration = sound). There are a variety of strengths that you can choose from. They range in color from light to dark.


This is a shoulder rest. It helps to prop up the instrument so its easier to play. This is optional to use. It depends on the build of the player.
